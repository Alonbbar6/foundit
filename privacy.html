Privacy Policy

FoundIt — Lost Pet Finder  |  Last updated June 2026

Overview

FoundIt ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core features:

  • Location: Used to display nearby pet sightings on the map and drop accurate location pins when reporting. Location is only accessed while the app is in use.
  • Photos: Images you voluntarily upload when reporting a lost pet or a stray animal sighting. Photos are stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • Pet information: Name, breed, color, and description of pets you report as lost. This information is visible to other users of the app.
  • Messages: In-app messages between users are stored in Firebase Firestore to enable communication. Messages are private between the two participants.
  • Anonymous identifier: The app uses anonymous authentication (no email or password required). A random device identifier is created automatically to associate your reports and messages.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it in a message or report.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display pet sightings and lost pet reports on the community map.
  • To send AI-powered match alerts when a sighting may match your lost pet.
  • To enable private in-app messaging between users.
  • To improve the app's performance and reliability.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage). Firebase is subject to Google's privacy and security standards. Data is stored in the United States.

In-App Purchases

Optional donations and business advertising purchases are processed by Apple through their secure payment system. We do not store payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment processing.

Children's Privacy

FoundIt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your anonymous identifier by contacting us at the email below. Since accounts are anonymous, data deletion removes all reports, sightings, and messages linked to your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
