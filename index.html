About FoundIt

FoundIt turns your neighborhood into a search party. Report lost pets, spot strays on the map, get AI-powered match alerts, and message neighbors directly — all in one app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost pet?

Tap Report at the bottom, select "My Pet is Lost," fill in your pet's details and a photo, and it will appear on the community map immediately.

How do I report a stray animal I spotted?

Tap Report, select "I Spotted a Stray," add a photo and description, and drop a pin on the map. Pet owners in the area will be notified.

How does AI matching work?

When a new sighting is reported, FoundIt automatically compares it against lost pet profiles in the area. If there's a strong match, the pet owner gets an alert instantly.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your personal details are never shared publicly. Messages are private between you and the other user. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is FoundIt free?

Yes, completely free to use. Optional donations help keep the app running. Local businesses can also purchase ad slots to support the app and reach pet owners in the community.

My pet was found — how do I remove it from the map?

Go to My Pets, open your pet's profile, and tap Mark as Found. Your pet will be removed from the community map.
